# Marketing Strategy
Target segments: knowledge workers, students, founders.
Positioning: practical sleep support companion in Telegram.
USP: fast recommendations + built-in alarm + feedback loop.
Channels:
- Owned: Telegram channel, product updates.
- Paid: Telegram Ads, niche influencer mentions.
- Earned: community reviews.
Message map: calm, transparent, no medical promises.
Telegram chosen for MVP due to distribution simplicity and low onboarding friction.
